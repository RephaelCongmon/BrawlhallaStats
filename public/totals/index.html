    What Are Global Statistics?
    - These statistics are statistics added across all Brawlhalla players across every device. Why aren't they updating or seem inaccuarate? Due to the API limit of 180 every 15 minutes, I can not refresh the data for every single player all the time to add to the stats.

    Therefore, I will update a player's stats and add the changes to the global stats when they are searched. If they have not been searched before, the website will add them automatically.

    Feel free to "pulse" your Brawlhalla stats to the Global Statistics by searching your name!
